Test Automation Workshops

Practical, hands-on training in creating maintainable tests for your game. Available for Unreal, Unity, and Godot, covering the tools already available in your engine.

What You'll Learn

  • The benefits of test automation for game development
  • The fail-fast-fail-hard mindset, and the benefits it brings even without automation
  • The available tooling in your game engine
  • The three most useful test types and when to use them
  • What types of tests to avoid
  • How to structure good tests to make them readable, maintainable and trustworthy

The Three Test Types

Asset Tests

Looking for configuration problems on-disk and scaling with new data

Smoke Tests

Pragmatically exploring the game from the player's point of view

Actor Tests

Rapidly testing gameplay logic in isolation

Two Formats

Choose the format that fits your team's schedule and goals. Both workshops can be tailored for your studio.

1-Day Crash Course

A fast, focused introduction to test automation for games. Covers the full syllabus in a single intensive day, giving your team the knowledge and confidence to start writing tests immediately.

  • Full syllabus covered in one day
  • In-person or remote
  • Ideal for teams who want to hit the ground running

3-Day Deep Dive

Everything in the crash course, plus time spent working directly on your project. You should end the workshop with tests that can go live directly, even if in a branch.

  • Full syllabus plus hands-on implementation
  • Leave with real, production-ready tests
  • 6 half-day mornings (one per week, remote) or 3 intensive in-person days
